**Join the humans of Vegas** at the unofficial opener of DEF CON. This Thursday Meat-Up is in the shade of Sunset Park, a quick ride from the LVCC. We stock the larder with burgers, dogs, and fixin’s. We rely on you for everything else: sides, drinks, volunteering, and donations.

With over 50 sq ft of heat, we have plenty of room on the grill for your personal creations. Contribute food and drinks, staff the grill, join supply runs, or donate to help cover cost. Everything left goes to the EFF. Be a part of what makes this cookout something to remember year after year. The only question is: **What are you bringing to Toxic BBQ?**

First time? Check out [How Does This Work](/volunteering). If you want to volunteer for Toxic BBQ in any capacity, check out this [Google Form](https://forms.gle/H4QMN56wChZwmQNQA).

Check out [toxicbbq.org](https://www.toxicbbq.org), find a flyer at an Info Booth, and watch for #ToxicBBQ on [the](https://defcon.social/tags/ToxicBBQ) [socials](https://bsky.app/hashtag/ToxicBBQ) for the latest news. 

## Improvements

- **The Line**: Last year's line was crazy! We’ve spent the off-season planning on how to make the line move faster. Let's get you to the food, and on the grass ASAP. And we're moving snacks, shade, and water into the line to help beat the heat.

## Events

- [Peppercon](https://discord.gg/URRBHAH): Push your spice tolerance to its breaking point. 
- [Homebrew Exhibition](/homebrew): Bring your finest bitters, ciders, wine, and assorted preparations for a fermented face off.
- Open Grill: Bring your favorite and most exotic cuts, then grill it just how you like on our expanded grill space.
- Line Food: Hot Ones flights, Cheesecon, and the like offer a bevy of interesting treats as you wait for the main event. 
- **Barbecued Hams**: A QSO party in the grass. Ham-curious? Come and ask questions wherever you see an antenna.
- Lab-grown Meat: maybe, and that's a big maybe, you could get your hands on some lab-grown meat.
- Have an Idea? It's a big park. Reach out and we'll find a way to make it happen

## Important Times
